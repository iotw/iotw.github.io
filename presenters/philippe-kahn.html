IoT & Wearables: Speakers

     

Philippe Kahn

Founder & CEO
Fullpower Technologies

Inventor, Technologist, visionary, sailor, mountaineer and jazz musician.

Kahn is credited with creating the first camera phone, being a pioneer for wearable technology intellectual property, and is the author of dozens of technology patents covering wearable, eyewear, smartphone, mobile, imaging, wireless, synchronization and medical technologies.

Most recently he founded Fullpower, leading the Wearable Sensing technology & IoT revolution with the MotionX and Sleeptracker Plug & Play Ecosystem for wearable & IoT. Sleeptracker® and MotionX® power market-leading wearable & IoT solutions from Nike, Jawbone and others. The Fullpower wearable & IoT patent portfolio includes more than 45 issued patents and more than 75 patents pending.
