Wallace Wu

VP, Software
Misfit Wearables

Coding since 13, gaming since 6, architecting Misfit software since the beginning. Doesn't own any shirts that are NOT hoodies.

Wallace leads the software engineering teams at Misfit where he works with commercial, design, and other cross-functional teams to deliver and maintain Misfit software products. Wallace also heads up China operations to develop and grow the Misfit team in China. Prior to Misfit, Wallace worked at Microsoft and Sonic Solutions, his experience ranges from delivering professional IT services to developing consumer software products.
